How will I manage my workload in this program

During the self-driven course, I will go over the structure of the sprint and set up a weekly study plan for myself. I will review my study plan each day to make sure I am on the right track. My goal is to work at least 8 hours per day and may be having some stretch to 10 to 12 hours if I feel left behind or If I want to explore more. I will take a 5 mins regular break every hour to stretch my body, and have some fresh air, these help me to refresh my brain, so that I can stay focus through out the day. I will also attend the weekly workshop and update my faciliator with my progress. For the bootcamp, I am commited that i will follow facilitator's instructions to complete the daily tasks. I will be fully commited to the projects, giving support and learn from my cohorts.

How and Where will I seek help in timely way?

When I get stuck, I will try to review the problem myself. If I still can not resolve it, I will search it on google or different forums, as this is the usually way when we working in the development industry. If I still have no hints, then I will seek help from my cohorts and facilitors by using slack or emailing them.

What do I expect from the facilitation team?

I would expect the facilitation team to give support when help is needed, as well as give me advices or share their experience on learning software development.
